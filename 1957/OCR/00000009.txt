Mrs. Calvert And Woodiey Birthday Singing
January 12, 1957
On Saturday night before the 2nd Sunday in January we met at the
home of Mr. and Mrs. Jim Woodleys to celebrate the birthday of Mr. Jim
Woodiey and Mrs. Luther Calvert. The house was called to order by Mr.
Woodiey. Leaders, Nelson Butler, Lera Pittman, Sam Tyree, Joe Myers,
