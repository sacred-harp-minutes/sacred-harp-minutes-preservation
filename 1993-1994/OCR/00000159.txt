singing the words to "Edmonds" to the tune of "Hallelujah" in anticipation of

tenors from the Charleston Group. Vicki and Joe pledged their troth in the
middle of the hollow square, surrounded by singers from several states
and friends and family invited for the occasion. Pastor Phil McCoy from the
Otterbein United Methodist Church officiated. After the couple repeated
their vows, Lee steinmetz offered a prayer for the couple's happiness and
led the convention and guests in singing a shape note version of "Blest Be
the Tie."
