LOUISIANA STATE SACRED HARP CONVENTION
Bethel Primitive Baptist Church
October 30. 1993
The fourth annual Louisiana State Sacred Harp Convention was held at
Bethel Primtive Baptist Church at Baton Rouge on October 30. The class
was called to order by Jim Fields. The morning prayer was led by Elder
Randy Myers, pastor of Bethel Church. Hugh McGraw gave a brief lesson on
Sacred Harp. Leaders: Jim Fields, 40; Hugh McGraw, 49, 497; Wallace
McKenzie, 49b, 48b; Rick McLain, 39; Harry Eskew, 63, 128; David
Young, 360, 64; John Del Re, 347, 42; Wanda Gregg, 28, 515; Ginny
Fields, 127; Mia McCauley, 38b, 45; Steve Morrison, 102; Nancy Van Den
Akker, 178, 480.
RECESS.
Jim Fields brought the class together leading song on page 457.
Acknowledgement was made of the courtesy of the Bethel Primitive Baptist
Church and its congregation in allowing the convention to be held here. Rick
McLain and several other members expressed the feeling of the
congregation, and extended an invitation for future conventions to meet
there. The class was then organized by electing the following officers:
Chairman Jim Fields; Vice Chairman, Wanda Gregg; Secretary, Nancy Van
Akker; Memorial Committee, Wallace McKenzie. Leaders: John Frank
Reeve. 81; Adrianna Hulscher, 159; Kelly Macklin, 171; Leyland Del Re,
207; Brian Howell, 59; John Del Re. 23.
