ST. MICHAEL CHURCH
Fruithurst, Alabama
June 20. 1993
The annual Alweine Memorial Sacred Harp Singing was held at St.
Michael Church on the third Sunday in June. The class was called together
by Jeff Sheppard leading songs on pages 32, 37b and 33b. Prayer was led
by Walter Hamrick. He then led song on page 147 in memory of Leo Kilgore.
There being so few leaders present it was decided that anyone in the class
or audience could call a number and they would sing it. that way everyone
got to choose a song. Singers in the class were; Bass. Jeff Sheppard, Billy
Joe Harris, ; Alto. Evelyn Harris. Treble; Ola Pruitt. Mary Florence
Smith, Alice Edwards, I. V. McWhorter; Tenor, Shelbie Sheppard, Cecil
Edwards, Walter Hamrick. "Novoices" Corine Laminack, Lucie McElroy,
Hester Edwards, Gladys Simmons. Ina Hamrick, Avis Kilgore and Reba Gay.
Jeff and Shelbie took time to explain some of the music as they sang it.
Number called were 146. 59. 56. 63. 31b. 30. 101. 100. 99. 143 for
Wille Mae and Curtis McLendon, 155.
