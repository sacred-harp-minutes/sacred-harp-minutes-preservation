CALHOUN COUNTY CONVENTION
Four Mile MT. Moriah Primitive Baptist Church
September 10, 1993
The seventy-ninth session of Calhoun County Sacred Harp Singing
Convention met on Saturday at Mt. Moriah Primitive Baptist Church and
continued in session thorugh Sunday. The class was called to order by B. J.
Harris leading song on page 30. The morning prayer was led by Rev
Howard Cook. B. J. Harris then led song on page 30b. Ruth Brown led 101
and 63. The convention then organized by electing the following officers:
Chairman, B. J. Harris; Vice Chairman, Ruth Brown; Secretary, Evelyn
Harris; Arranging Committee and Memorial Committee. Hester Edwards
and Pearl Guier. Leaders; Evelyn Harris. 47, 47b; Harvey Austin, 556,
108; Pearl Guier. 59. 143; B. M. Smith. 77b. 273; Lou Cotnery. 212,
358; Milton Oliver. 303. 321b; Linda Thomas. 33b. 275b; Shelton
Williamson. 186. 222 ; Jewel Wootten, 208, 299.
