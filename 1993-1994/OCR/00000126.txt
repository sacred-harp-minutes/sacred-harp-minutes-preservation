CHATTAHOOCHEE CONVENTION
Wilson's Chapel Church. - Carrollton. Georgia
July 31 and August I. 1993
The Chattahoochee Sacred Harp Musical convention convened at
Wilsons Chapel Church near Carrollton. Gerogia. on Saturday morning,
July 31. 1993. and continued in session through Sunday, August 1. 1993.
The Conventtion opened with singing and prayer. Chairman, Sherie Taylor
led song on pages 73 and 77. Prayer was led by Everette Denney. The
officers then traditonally led as follows; David McGukin. V. Chairman.
